About JRS Coaching

JRS Coaching, also known as JRS Tutorials, is a leading institute in Varanasi established in 1994 by Amrendra Kumar Jha and others. It prepares students for IIT-JEE (Main & Advanced) and NEET-PMT.

Key Features

  • Experienced and committed full-time faculty
  • Focus on Physics and Chemistry excellence
  • Systematic and complete syllabus coverage
  • Regular topic-wise and combined tests
  • Doubt-solving sessions after class
  • Emphasis on solving approach over rote learning
  • Classroom and online coaching available
  • EMI payment and fee concession options
  • One of the top institutes in Purvanchal region

Courses & Fees

  • IIT-JEE (Main + Advanced) – ₹111,500 to ₹117,500
  • NEET-PMT – Around ₹105,500
  • Pre-foundation (Class 8-9) – Olympiad, NTSE, KVPY Prep
