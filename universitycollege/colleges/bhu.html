Admission Process

Admissions to Banaras Hindu University are conducted through national-level entrance exams:

  • Undergraduate (UG): Admission via CUET-UG conducted by NTA. Candidates must apply through CUET and choose BHU as their preferred university.
  • Postgraduate (PG): Admission through CUET-PG or direct BHU process depending on course.
  • Ph.D. Programs: Based on Research Entrance Test (RET) and interview conducted by BHU.

After results, candidates must complete BHU counselling via the official admission portal at bhuonline.in.
