About IIT (BHU) Varanasi

IIT (BHU) Varanasi is one of India‚Äôs oldest engineering institutions, tracing its origins to the Banaras Engineering College (1919). It became an IIT in 2012 and is located within the Banaras Hindu University (BHU) campus in Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh. Known for its rich heritage and academic excellence, IIT BHU offers world-class education, research, and innovation opportunities in science, technology, and engineering.

The institute blends traditional Indian values with modern technological advancements, preparing students to become global leaders and innovators.

Courses Offered

  • B.Tech: Admission through JEE Advanced
  • Integrated Dual Degree (B.Tech + M.Tech)
  • M.Tech: Admission through GATE
  • Ph.D.: Research in advanced engineering and sciences
  • MBA: Offered under the School of Management Sciences

Admission Process

  • B.Tech / Dual Degree: Based on JEE Advanced Rank through JoSAA counselling.
  • M.Tech: Admission through GATE scores and CCMT counselling.
  • Ph.D.: Through Departmental Written Test and Interview.
  • MBA: Based on CAT scores and group discussion/interview process.

For more details and registration, visit the official site: www.iitbhu.ac.in/admissions
