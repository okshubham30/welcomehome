About MGKVP, Varanasi

Mahatma Gandhi Kashi Vidyapith (MGKVP) is a state university located in the holy city of Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh. Established in 1921 during India‚Äôs freedom movement, it played a major role in promoting Indian education free from British control. The university was renamed after Mahatma Gandhi in 1995.

It offers a broad range of academic programs across Arts, Science, Commerce, Education, Law, and Social Sciences. MGKVP has more than 350 affiliated colleges spread across various districts of Uttar Pradesh.

Courses Offered

  • Undergraduate Programs: B.A., B.Sc., B.Com, BBA, BCA, LL.B., B.Ed
  • Postgraduate Programs: M.A., M.Sc., M.Com, MBA, LL.M, M.Ed
  • Doctoral and Research Programs (Ph.D.)
  • Professional Courses: Journalism, Social Work, Library Science, Fine Arts

Admission Process

  • UG Courses: Admission based on marks obtained in 10+2 or entrance exams conducted by the university.
  • PG Courses: Merit-based or through MGKVP Entrance Test.
  • Ph.D. Programs: Admission through Research Entrance Test (RET) and interview.

Applications are accepted online via the official portal: mgkvponline.org.in

Fees and Scholarships

  • Affordable fee structure suitable for all students.
  • Scholarships for SC/ST/OBC and meritorious students as per government norms.
  • Merit-cum-means scholarships for economically weaker students.
  • Various state-level and central government schemes supported by the university.

Campus Life and Facilities

  • Spacious academic blocks and green campus area.
  • Central library with thousands of books and digital resources.
  • Separate hostels for boys and girls.
  • Playgrounds, gymnasium, and sports facilities.
  • Student cultural and annual events organized regularly.
  • Wi-Fi enabled campus and computer labs.
