About the Temple

Kashi Vishwanath Temple, located in Varanasi (Uttar Pradesh), is one of the twelve Jyotirlingas and holds immense cultural, spiritual, and historical significance.

  • Dedicated to Lord Shiva
  • Located in Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh
  • Considered one of the holiest Hindu pilgrimage sites
  • Recently redeveloped under the Kashi Vishwanath Corridor project
  • Millions of devotees visit every year

Location & Highlights

City: Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh

Nearby: Ganga Ghat, Dashashwamedh Ghat, Sarnath

The temple is a major pilgrimage spot with ancient architecture, spiritual atmosphere, and easy access to Ganga Aarti and nearby tourist attractions.
