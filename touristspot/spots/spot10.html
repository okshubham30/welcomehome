About Sarveshwar Mandir

Sarveshwar Mandir is a sacred and ancient temple located near the Assi area of Varanasi, dedicated to Lord Shiva, revered here as Lord Sarveshwar Mahadev. The temple is surrounded by peace and spiritual aura, attracting devotees seeking divine blessings and inner calm. It is believed that Lord Shiva himself manifested here to bless his devotees.

History & Significance

  • The temple has been worshipped since ancient times as a significant Shiva Peeth.
  • It is believed to be one of the sacred points connecting the cosmic energy of Kashi.
  • Many sages and saints performed meditation here, making it a center of deep spiritual vibration.

Architecture & Structure

  • The temple features traditional Nagara-style architecture with a towering spire (Shikhara).
  • Built using sandstone and marble, giving it an elegant divine appearance.
  • The sanctum (Garbhagriha) enshrines a black Shiva Lingam known as Sarveshwar Mahadev.
  • The surrounding area is filled with trees and peaceful ambiance, ideal for meditation.
