About the School

Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalaya, Varanasi is a co-educational residential school established by the Government of India under the Navodaya Vidyalaya Samiti. It provides quality education to talented children predominantly from rural areas of Varanasi district. The school is affiliated to CBSE and offers classes from VI to XII.

Admission Process

  • Main entry through the Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalaya Selection Test (JNVST) for Class VI.
  • Additional lateral entry tests for Class IX and XI, subject to vacancy.
  • Application forms are released online at navodaya.gov.in.
  • No admission through donation or direct admission ‚Äî purely merit based.

Facilities

  • Well-equipped science and computer laboratories.
  • Smart classrooms and digital learning through Samsung Smart School initiative.
  • Library, art and music rooms, and multi-purpose hall.
  • Sports ground for football, volleyball, athletics and indoor games.

Hostel Life

The school offers separate hostels for boys and girls with resident staff supervision. Students are provided nutritious meals, health care, and opportunities for co-curricular development.
