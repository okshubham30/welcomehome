About Atal Residential School Varanasi

Atal Residential School (ARS) Varanasi is a state government initiative established to provide quality education to children of labourers and economically weaker sections. The school operates as a fully residential campus with modern facilities, focusing on holistic development including academics, sports, and moral education.

It aims to bridge the gap between rural and urban education by offering a CBSE-standard curriculum and well-trained teachers. The medium of instruction is bilingual (Hindi & English) to ensure better comprehension and confidence among students.

Admission Process

  • Admissions are based on merit and eligibility criteria set by the Uttar Pradesh Government.
  • Applicants must be children of registered labourers or economically weaker families.
  • Application forms are available through the official Atal Residential School portal.
  • Selection includes written assessment and document verification.
