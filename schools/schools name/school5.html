About the School

Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalaya, Chandauli was established in 1987 by the Government of India under the Navodaya Vidyalaya Samiti. It is a fully residential, co-educational institution affiliated with the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE).

The school is located at Dharahara in a serene, eco-friendly environment surrounded by nature. JNV Chandauli provides free quality education, lodging, and boarding to talented rural students of the district.

Admission Process

  • Admission in Class VI through the Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalaya Selection Test (JNVST).
  • Lateral entry for Classes IX and XI subject to available seats.
  • Online registration and details available at navodaya.gov.in.
  • No capitation fee or donation accepted ‚Äî admission purely on merit basis.

Facilities

  • Smart classrooms and modern teaching aids.
  • Science labs (Physics, Chemistry, Biology) and Computer lab.
  • Library with reference, fiction, and periodicals section.
  • Hostels for boys and girls with warden supervision.
  • Sports fields for football, volleyball, cricket, and athletics.
  • Medical room and hygienic dining hall.

Campus & Environment

The school campus spans around 30 acres of lush greenery. It provides a perfect atmosphere for learning, creativity, and all-round development. The residential environment encourages teamwork, self-discipline, and cultural harmony among students.

Academics

JNV Chandauli offers education from Class VI to XII. The curriculum follows the CBSE pattern. The school focuses on conceptual learning, STEM activities, and value-based education. Streams offered at the senior secondary level include:

  • Science (with Computer Science or Biology)
  • Commerce
  • Humanities

Co-curricular & Sports

  • Sports meets at cluster, regional, and national levels.
  • Art, music, and cultural competitions.
  • Science exhibitions, quizzes, and literary clubs.
  • Yoga and physical training for mental well-being.
